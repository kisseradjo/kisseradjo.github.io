About me

My profession as an artist started off on a rather bumpy note. Being an artist has been one of the scariest things I have had to be so resolute about. This resolve is largely due to my staunch belief in the possibility that art possesses to expand our notion of freedom. I am an independent artist who is interested in understanding drawing. I recently completed a graduate program in Fine Art at the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology in Kumasi - Ghana. My hobbies are lived out through every bit of my profession. And each day provides an opportunity to live my dreams however scary. I have never overlooked the field of science and technology as one that could hold answers to understanding drawing anew. In fact, it has been one of things I have held on to as having great capacity to expand our conception of drawing. This belief is the reason for taking up a course in programming.

What happens when a drawing, which is essentially a connection of points, is lifted off a paper, a canvas or any surface and begins to live among us?



Writing has always been integral to my practice. Though a lot of the ideas within my articles on tumblr have been updated, it is still good to see how far i have come as a person.
